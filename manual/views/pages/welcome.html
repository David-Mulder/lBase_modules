Welcome to lBase for lack of a better name

lBase is a framework inspired by the best of CodeIgniter and Laravel without the level of complexity that comes with Laravel.

this.app and this.mod

Anywhere you are all the application functioned (as outlined in the rest of the manual) can be acessed through this.app and the module you are currently in through this.mod. Additionally lBase core methods can be accessed through this.lbase, however this should be rarely needed.
